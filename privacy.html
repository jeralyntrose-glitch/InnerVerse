Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 17, 2025

1. Overview

Axis Mind ("we," "our," or "us") provides a PDF knowledge base and AI-powered question-answering service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Documents & Content:

  • PDF files you upload
  • Text content you submit for PDF generation
  • YouTube video URLs you provide for transcription
  • Questions you ask through our chat interface

Technical Data:

  • IP addresses (for rate limiting and security)
  • API request logs (retained for 30 days)
  • Usage metrics (number of requests, error rates)
  • Browser type and device information

Cookies:

  • We use minimal cookies for theme preferences (light/dark mode)
  • No tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process and store your uploaded documents in our vector database
  • Generate AI-powered answers to your questions
  • Transcribe YouTube videos into searchable PDFs
  • Monitor API usage and prevent abuse through rate limiting
  • Improve our services and fix technical issues

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI - For AI embeddings, chat responses, and text processing (Privacy Policy)
  • Pinecone - For vector database storage of document embeddings (Privacy Policy)
  • Replit - For application hosting (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Drive API - For optional file uploads (Privacy Policy)

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Document embeddings are stored in Pinecone's secure cloud database
  • API keys and authentication tokens are encrypted at rest
  • We implement rate limiting (100 requests/hour) to prevent abuse
  • API logs are automatically deleted after 30 days

6. Data Sharing

We DO NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Use your documents for training AI models
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Make your uploaded documents publicly accessible

We ONLY share data with:

  • Third-party services necessary to operate (OpenAI, Pinecone, Replit)
  • Law enforcement if legally required

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data through our chat commands (list docs, show doc [id])
  • Delete your documents anytime using chat command delete doc [id]
  • Delete all your documents using the "Delete All Files" button
  • Request a copy of your data by contacting us

8. Data Retention

  • Documents: Stored until you delete them manually
  • API Logs: Automatically deleted after 30 days
  • Chat History: Not permanently stored (session-based only)

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. API-Specific Information

For Custom GPT Users:

  • Questions asked through Custom GPT are processed through our API
  • We log API requests for rate limiting and security (30-day retention)
  • Your OpenAI account handles Custom GPT interactions
  • We do not have access to your OpenAI account information

API Security:

  • All API requests use HTTPS encryption
  • Rate limiting prevents abuse (100 requests/hour)
  • API usage costs are tracked and displayed to the service owner only

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, please use the contact information provided in the application.

By using Axis Mind, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
